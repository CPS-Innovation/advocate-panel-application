Help with references

It is the Applicant’s responsibility to secure a referee’s agreement to provide a reference and to ensure that the reference is submitted by the deadline.

The deadline for receipt of references is 30th September for applications submitted during the annual September application window. Applicants should ensure referees are given sufficient notice (28 days) to allow them sufficient time to write and submit the reference.
Where references are required (or optional), provision is made to supply the appropriate referee details. Referees will be invited by email to access this service and submit a reference in support of your application.

References can be obtained from any person whom the applicant believes best supports their application and is prepared to stand as a referee. The list below provides examples of suitable referees for your application & level.

  • Pupil Supervisor
  • Heads of Chambers
  • Chambers Advocacy Trainers

Referee details

Please provide the details of the referee you wish to use in support of your application.

Providing an incorrect email address can cause delays with your reference. CPS referees use the format Firstname.Surname@cps.gov.uk

Referee role

The role or capacity in which a referee is acting For CPS referees you should select 'CPS Lawyer'.

Applicant introduction

The text to be sent to the referee in the reference request. You should state why you are applying, highlight any cases you have worked on together or other information that could act as a reference point for your referee.
