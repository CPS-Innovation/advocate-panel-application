Use this service to apply to join a CPS advocate panel for:

  • Crown Court, if you’re a barrister or a solicitor with higher rights of audience
  • Magistrates’ and Youth Court, if you’re a solicitor

Before you start

To register or sign in you'll need access to your email and mobile phone.

To apply your the first time you’ll need your:

  • bar reference number of Solicitor Regulation Authority (SRA) number

Check what examples of work, evidence and references you'll need to apply for:

  • general crime or RASSO panels
  • specialist panels - these are serious crime, counter terrorism, fraud, proceeds of crime and extradition
  • Magistrates’ and Youth Court panels

You must not include identifying case information in your application.
